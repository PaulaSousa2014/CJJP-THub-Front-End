FAQ's

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What is T-Hub?

T-Hub is an application designed exclusively for T-Systems employees. It serves as a platform to facilitate communication, collaboration, and networking among employees, with the goal of fostering stronger relationships and team cohesion within the organization.

Who can access the T-Hub app?

The T-Hub app is exclusively for T-Systems employees. It is designed to facilitate networking and communication among employees within the organization.

How do I create an account on T-Hub?

To create an account on T-Hub, you need to be a T-Systems employee. Please use your corporate credentials to log in to the app. If you encounter any issues during the account creation process, please reach out to the T-Hub support team.

How can I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information by navigating to your profile settings within the T-Hub app. From there, you can edit your personal details, such as your workplace, office location, and other relevant information.

How can I post on the common feed?

To post on the common feed, go to the main screen of the T-Hub app and look for the option to create a new post. Click on it, and you will be able to share your thoughts, ideas, or any other information with the entire T-Hub community. You can also comment on and react to posts from your colleagues.

How can I connect with my colleagues on T-Hub?
You can connect with your colleagues on T-Hub by sending friend requests. Use the search feature within the app to find your colleagues' profiles and send them a friend request. Once they accept your request, you will be able to interact and communicate with them through the app.
How can I join or create groups on T-Hub?

To join a group on T-Hub, use the search feature to find the desired group and send a request to join. If the group you're looking for doesn't exist, you can create your own group within the app. Specify the category and purpose of the group, invite colleagues, and start engaging in group discussions.

Can I create different types of groups on T-Hub?

Absolutely! T-Hub allows you to create groups in three different categories: Games, Activities and Social.

How can I access the T-Hub support team if I need assistance?

If you need any assistance or have questions regarding the T-Hub app, please reach out to the T-Hub support team. You can find their contact information in the app's settings or refer to the provided documentation and resources for further assistance.
